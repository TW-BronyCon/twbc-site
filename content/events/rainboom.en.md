As a Brony, are the catchy tunes of My Little Pony constantly stuck in your head? Do you find yourself on an endless loop listening to MLP music? The late-night music party will feature 3 DJs taking turns on stage to hype up the crowd. Have you reviewed your favorite MLP songs yet?

## DJ Line-up & Event Highlights

- **Live MLP DJ Sets**  
  Featuring 3 community DJs spinning custom MLP remixes and electronic tracks. Relive your favorite musical moments with high-energy beats!

- **Vibrant Visuals & Lights**  
  Dynamic lighting and background visuals synced perfectly to the rhythm of pony music.

- **Brony Community Sing-along**  
  Dance and sing along with fellow Bronies. Feel the magic of friendship in every beat!

## Party Guidelines

- Light sticks, glow gear, and glowing cosplay are highly welcome!
- Stay hydrated and take breaks when needed.
- Respect others and follow staff instructions to ensure a safe and fun environment for all.
